To preview a template after you have created it, click on the template name in the Templates table. 

<figure>
<img src="https://hilos-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/documentation/userdocs/using-hilos/reference/templates/previewing-a-template/img1.png" alt="Preview a template" />
<figcaption>Preview a template</figcaption>
</figure>

This will open the template in the Preview Editor where you can preview the template and test its variables.

<img src="https://hilos-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/documentation/userdocs/using-hilos/reference/templates/previewing-a-template/img2.png" alt="Preview a template" />
