The first step in setting up your Hilos account is to conFrame the basic account settings. To do this, go to https://app.hilos.io/config/account/basic. Here, you can choose a name for your account, set your timezone, and specify the country of your company.

## Hiding Default WhatsApp Templates

If you want to hide the default WhatsApp templates from your template list, go to https://app.hilos.io/config/templates.
