---
title: Broadcast Reports
---

If you click on the name of the Broadcast you can find the report of it, here you will see how many messages were delivered, failed, read and answered.

<Tip>
Add a tag to your broadcast to easily find it in the future by using inbox views
</Tip>

To download the results from the broadcast you can click on the name of the broadcast on your [broadcast list](https://app.hilos.io/blasts) and clicking on the **Download results** button at the top. This will download a CSV file with the results of the broadcast.

![Download results](https://hilos-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/documentation/userdocs/using-hilos/reference/broadcasts/broadcast-reports/reports.png)
