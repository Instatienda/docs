To customize your team's Inbox, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your inbox settings">
    Go to [settings > Inbox config](https://app.hilos.io/config/account/inbox).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your inbox">
    Customize the following settings to your linking:
    - Add the agent's name before each message.
    - Choose whether agents can see all conversations or only the ones assigned to them.
    - Set a time limit to automatically close conversations.
    - Automatically archive conversations when marked as closed.
    - Send a customer satisfaction question after each conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    After making the desired changes, click on **Save changes**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
