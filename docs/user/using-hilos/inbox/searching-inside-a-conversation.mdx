---
title: Searching inside a conversation
---

To find messages inside a conversation first go to your [Inbox](https://app.hilos.io/inbox/) and click on a conversation. Then click on the **Search** button on the top of the conversation, next to the conversation status.

![Search button](https://hilos-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/documentation/userdocs/using-hilos/reference/inbox/searching-inside-a-conversation/img1.png)

On the sidebar that appeared type the text you wish to search for, any matching query will appear on the sidebar.

![Search results](https://hilos-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/documentation/userdocs/using-hilos/reference/inbox/searching-inside-a-conversation/img2.png)
