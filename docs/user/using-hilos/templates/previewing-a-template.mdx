---
title: Previewing a Template
---

To preview a template after you have created it, click on the template name in the Templates table. 

![Preview a template](https://hilos-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/documentation/userdocs/using-hilos/reference/templates/previewing-a-template/img1.png)
*Preview a template*

This will open the template in the Preview Editor where you can preview the template and test its variables.
