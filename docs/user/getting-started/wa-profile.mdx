Go to the [WhatsApp Business Manager](https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/home) and click on the number you wish to edit its profile.

![Profile 1](https://hilos-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/mintlify/getting-started/wa-profile/profile1.jpg)

On the window that opens, you will be able to change the profile picture, bio, apply for a green verification badge, change the category, and modify links shown.
Make the desired changes to your profile, and click on **Save Changes**.

![Profile 2](https://hilos-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/mintlify/getting-started/wa-profile/profile2.jpg)

## Changing Your Display Name

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a phone number">
    Go to the [WhatsApp phone numbers page](https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/phone-numbers) and click on the pencil next to the display name on the number you wish to modify.
    ![Profile 3](https://hilos-media.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/mintlify/getting-started/wa-profile/profile3.jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the display name">
    Enter the new display name that you wish to use for your WhatsApp Business account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Await WhatsApp name verification">
    Await WhatsApp name verification
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let us know">
    Once your name is verified, [let us know](mailto:hey@hilos.io) to complete the change on our platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>
